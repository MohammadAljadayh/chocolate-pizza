For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugarand stir to dissolve. Halve all
of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemom jucie. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until
you have a chuunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completly

Ice cream: in a small pot over medium heat, combine milk,and granulated sugar until sugar is completly
dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge
and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine,poure liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer
instruction,i.e,let spin and thicken for 20 minuets befor adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture and the nuts.
continue to process for +/- 10 minuets. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware.freeze for
at least two hours before serving.
